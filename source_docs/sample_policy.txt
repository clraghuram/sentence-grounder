# Company AI Usage and LLM Grounding Policy

## Section 1: Introduction
All employees must ensure that any output generated by Large Language Models (LLMs) or AI assistants is properly validated against source documents before use in official communications or decisions.

## Section 2: Grounding Requirements
2.1 Every factual claim produced by an LLM must be traceable to an approved internal document (PDF, DOCX, or policy wiki page).

2.2 Manual validation or use of approved automated grounding tools is mandatory for high-stakes content.

2.3 The preferred method for quick validation is to use a fast document search tool that can scan all policy PDFs and return exact matching paragraphs with page numbers.

## Section 3: Prohibited Uses
Employees shall not rely solely on LLM responses for:
- Financial reporting numbers
- Legal interpretations
- Safety or compliance statements
without performing source verification.

## Section 4: Recommended Tools
The company approved tool "FastDocSearch" allows indexing of all policy documents once and then instant search for any phrase from an LLM answer.

Example query: "employees must ensure that any output generated by Large Language Models"

This must return the relevant section from this or other policy files.
